WHAT IS ABACUS?
An abacus is a calculation tool used by sliding counters along rods or grooves, used to perform mathematical functions.

WHY SHOULD MY CHILD LEARN ABACUS?
Abacus learning not only helps with the basic calculations like addition, multiplication, subtraction, and division, it also helps in effective counting of decimal points, negative numbers, etc. Look at the video for a better understanding.
